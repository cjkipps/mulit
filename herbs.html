Herbs

Herbs are the heroes of the garden world. Generally, they are simple to grow, beautiful to look at, and useful in the kitchen. You can plant herbs in the garden or in containers. They also provide a structure for your garden. Come in to see our selection and learn how to grow and use these hardworking treasures. We'll be happy to help you find the right selection of herbs for your kitchen garden.

This is Peppermint, one of our holiday favorities. It thrives in moist soil and is great for digestion.

A favorite among chefs, Basil is great for garnish. Basil loves the sun and the heat.

Parsely is well loved italian dishes, and has a peppery taste. Great for a morning omelette.
